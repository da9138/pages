About slimes!

What is a slime?

A slime is a toy that can be used for sress releaf and ASMR. It was manufactured by Mattel, and introduced in Feburary 1976.
Slime can be used as a sensory play tool for children, as it provides a unique tactile experience that can help develop sensory skills.

What's slime made of?

-Glue (White,Glitter,Coloured or Clear.)
- Borax, Luandry detergent or water and baking soda.
- Later also charms, glitter (if not in the glue) colour (also if not in the glue) will be added into the slime.
